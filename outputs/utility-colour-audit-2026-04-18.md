## Thresholds

- **WCAG non-text contrast ≥ 3:1** (WCAG 2.1 AA 1.4.11).
- **CIEDE2000 ΔE ≥ 15** for distinguishability at a glance under normal and simulated dichromatic vision.
- Dichromacy simulation: culori `filterDeficiencyDeuter(1)` and `filterDeficiencyProt(1)` — full severity.

**Casing semantics:** the "effective contrast" for a surface is `max(fill-contrast, casing-contrast)`. When the fill falls below 3:1 (e.g. gas yellow vs a white card), the slate-900 casing underneath carries legibility instead. Reported as PASS when either component clears the bar.

**Expected non-failure:** `electricity` is semantic red, so low ΔE vs `--destructive` is by design and excluded from its failure list.
